About

Problem Statement

In order to have an official golf handicap within Australia, a golfer is required to have a $200 annual Golflink subscription. However many casual golfers would like to know their handicaps without having to pay this fee. Some golfers may have tried to keep track of their own handicap over time however this can sometimes be difficult as there are a number of variables involved in the handicap calculation.

Solution

The aim of this web application is to allow casual golfers to track their own and their partners handicaps over time. This is done by providing an easy to use interface with minimal input required from the user.

Method of Calculation

The method used to calculate handicaps is taken from https://www.golf.org.au/howtocalculateahandicap. It should be noted that since the daily scratch rating of a course is not published I have taken the average scratch rating for that course as specified here https://www.golf.org.au/australian-slope-ratings/state/WA
